The Baliuag University EdTech Day 2025 happens on July 3, 2025, at Baliuag University, Baliwag, Central Luzon. This event is a significant occasion for the university, as it marks the first EdTech Day, which is a celebration of the university's legacy and a showcase of its commitment to transforming higher education in the Philippines. The event will feature tech-powered exhibits and thought leadership talks, aiming to explore the evolving role of digital innovation in education.
